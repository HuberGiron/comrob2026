REGISTRATION INFORMATION

XXVIII Mexican Congress of Robotics

Dear attendees of the XXVIII Mexican Congress of Robotics (COMRob 2026), below you will find information related to the registration process.

Quick Guide

  • Check the applicable fee according to your category (presenter, student, etc.) on Fees.
  • IBERO students or faculty members should consider a 10% discount on the corresponding fee.
  • If you are a Presenter-Author, please have the ID number(s) of your accepted paper(s) ready.
  • The Student Presenter fee includes ONE accepted paper for presentation.
  • The Researcher/Professional Presenter fee includes TWO accepted papers for presentation and the possibility of ONE paper to be published in IEEE Xplore (if the corresponding registration and paper acceptance are obtained).
  • If a presenter exceeds the included number of papers, they must pay $2,500.00 MXN per extra paper:
    • Student Presenter: maximum total of 2 papers.
    • Researcher/Professional Presenter: maximum total of 3 papers.
  • If you require an invoice, please have your complete billing information ready.
  • For logistical purposes (final technical program, gala dinner, welcome kit), registration is requested by:
    • Presenters: no later than October 2.
    • Other attendees including gala dinner and kit: no later than October 15.
